John Austin Roberts

After graduating from the Full Stack Web Development Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, I was hired on as a Teaching Assistant for their Data Science and Vizualization Bootcamp. I am now on my second class rotation as a TA, and have picked up many valuable skills, from D3 and Tableau to VBA and MatPlotLib.

As a full stack developer, data scientist, and video game enthusiast, I like to spend my time learning new concepts about coding and video game mechanics. I enjoy spending my off time playing a few of my favorite video games, as they are my true passion. I am in the beginning stages of developing my own game with a co-worker.

Before my time as a TA, I was working in restaurants for many years, both as a server and in management roles. Through those years, I gained valuable skills, such as hosptality service, problem solving, time management, and customer service. While these jobs have been enjoyable, I am very excited to now be pursuing a position as a web developer.
